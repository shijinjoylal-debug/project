MECHANICS

Quantum mechanics is the branch of physics that studies the behaviour of matter and energy at the atomic scale, which differs from the rules of classical physics that govern everyday objects. Quantum mechanics reveals a world where particles can exist in multiple states at once, move through barriers, and be connected across vast distances through entanglement.

This fascinating field forms the foundation of modern technology, It challenges our understanding of reality and continues to inspire new discoveries about the univer's most fundamental nature .To Learn more Take a deep dive into each page
